## 01 · Boulevard of White Fire
mood: Brother's Keeper energy · white jacket · chrome heat · palms · destiny
about: The opening statement of the album. Sets the whole world in motion: humid midnight air, glowing asphalt, police-light shimmer and a man driving straight into the myth of the city. The ignition point where style, danger and fate first lock together.

## 02 · Calderone Heat
mood: Latin-shadow pressure · revenge · cartel ghosts · blood-warm ocean air
about: A hotter, more personal descent into the underworld. Carries the pulse of revenge, cartel tension and tropical night pressure, where every deal feels loaded and every memory burns twice.

## 03 · Smuggler Moon
mood: Luxury danger · speedboats · harbor haze · expensive corruption · glossy decay
about: The album's seductive corruption piece: sleek, expensive and quietly rotten underneath the shine. Moves through marina lights, fast boats, linen jackets and polished lies.

## 04 · Code of the Burning Shore
mood: Honor · restraint · deadly calm · black water · neon and salt fire
about: A colder and more disciplined turn. Built around honor, control and the silent weight of decisions made without spectacle. Masculinity sharpened by restraint, danger and principle.

## 05 · Ghosts Where the Buses Don't Run
mood: Broken justice · motel dread · obsession · city breathing through the barrel of a gun
about: One of the darkest cuts on the album. Drifts through obsession, broken justice and sleepless neon paranoia, like an old case that never really ended.

## 06 · This City Tastes Like Vice
mood: Humid neon seduction · beautiful poison · fatal pull · never really leave
about: Treats the city itself like a lover: dazzling, dangerous and impossible to quit. All humid air, pink reflections and beautiful poison.

## 07 · Evan's Last Summer
mood: Memory · brotherhood · tenderness · loss · warm salt air
about: One of the emotional core pieces. Steps away from pure crime-noir into memory, friendship and the ache of someone who still lives inside the weather of the past. Warm, melodic and deeply human.

## 08 · Mercy Under Neon Skies
mood: Midnight prayer · grace · glamour · guilt · humid confession
about: A spiritual-noir turning point. Holds the tension between sin, glamour and the quiet hope that something like grace can still survive under artificial light.

## 09 · Shattered in the Mirrorlight
mood: Identity fracture · hotel-room neon · wounded selfhood · deep emotional turn
about: About broken identity, fragile selfhood and the feeling of looking into hotel-room neon and no longer fully recognizing the man reflected there. Dreamlike, wounded and intimate.

## 10 · Freefall at Dawn
mood: Sunrise after damage · survival · horizon · salt light · final comedown
about: The emotional finale. Arrives after all the damage, speed, heat and fractured memory with a sense of battered grace. Survival itself can still feel luminous.

## 11 · Ocean Drive After Midnight
mood: Instrumental bonus · TV-intro rush · analog hooks · skyline glow · cool melancholy
about: A bonus instrumental built as a direct rush of neon-coastal momentum. Distills the album's visual DNA into pure motion: skyline shimmer, wet streets, analog hooks, speed and cool melancholy.
